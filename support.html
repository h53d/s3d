Shine3D Support

Purpose:
Shine3D is a lightweight 3D visualization tool: no registration, no uploads, and no internet required. Everything works offline, locally, and privately.

Key Features

  • Broad Format Support: Seamlessly load and display a wide range of 3D formats, including STL, OBJ, GLB, 3MF, USDZ, 3DS, 3DM, PLY, XYZ, AMF, VOX, VTK, VTP, and FBX.
  • Dynamic Environments & Toon Shading: Multiple preset scenarios and stylized shaders to make your models and anime figures truly "pop" with character-accurate lighting.
  • Custom Branding & Logos: Personalize your presentation by uploading your own logo image as the stand floor.
  • High-Res Capture: Generate crisp PNG screenshots, cinematic MP4 recordings, and universal GIF animations for effortless sharing.
  • Local & Lightning Fast: Optimized for performance, running locally on your device for an instant, lag-free experience.
  • Professional Integrity: No ads. No tracking. No data collection. Just a clean, efficient tool.

Technical Specifications

  • Requires iOS 16, macOS 12 or later.
  • Optimized for high performance and resource efficiency.
  • Works offline ensuring complete data privacy.
