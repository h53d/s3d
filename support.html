Shine3D

Local 3D Model Show

Purpose:
Shine3D is a lightweight 3D visualization tool: no registration, no uploads, and no internet required. Everything works offline, locally, and privately.

Key Features

Broad Format Support

Seamlessly load and display STL, OBJ, GLB, 3MF, USDZ, 3DS, 3DM, PLY, XYZ, AMF, VOX, VTK, VTP, and FBX.

Dynamic Environments & Toon Shading

Choose from preset scenarios or stylized shaders to make your models "pop" with character-accurate lighting.

Custom Branding & Logos

Personalize your presentation by using your own logo as the floor of the display stand.

High-Res Image, MP4 & GIF

Generate crisp PNG screenshots, cinematic MP4 videos, and universal GIFs for quick and lightweight sharing.

Local & Lightning Fast

Optimized for an instant, lag-free experience running locally on your device.

Professional Integrity

No ads. No tracking. No data collection. Built for professionals.

Technical Specifications

  • Requires iOS 16, macOS 12 or later.
  • Optimized for high performance and resource efficiency.
  • 100% Offline: No network access required, ensuring complete data privacy.
