Privacy Policy for Shine3D

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

Shine3D values your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled. Spoiler: we don’t collect it. Here's what that really means.

1. No Data Collection

Shine3D does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens locally on your device. That means:

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cloud uploads
  • No third-party integrations
  • No data sharing with us or others

Your photos, models, and exports stay on your device — unless you choose to share them with others.

2. Offline Processing

All features in Shine3D run entirely offline. We never upload your files to any server. Your creative work remains completely under your control.

3. Local Storage Only

Your data is only saved temporarily on your local device while using the app. We don’t have access to these files, and they’re never shared unless you initiate the sharing yourself.

4. No Third-Party SDKs

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or any third-party services. No tracking. No fingerprinting. No hidden surprises.

5. Your Rights

Since we don’t collect any data, there’s nothing for you to request, delete, or revoke. However, if you have questions or concerns, we’re happy to answer them.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this policy. Continued use of Shine3D after any modifications signifies acceptance of these changes.
